INTRODUCTION

Home Comforts Tea was established in 2011 and is involved in the blending and packing of tea for the local retail market and is also involved in the Bulk trading of tea for the Export Market.

Home Comforts sources and selects only the highest quality teas from the finest tea gardens located in the cool highland plantations of Kenya. Tea from this region is world reknowned for its unique taste and rich flavor.

The fresh tea is thereafter blended and packed immediately under the Home Comforts Tea registered brand name under strict hygenic conditions using fully automated modern equipment.
